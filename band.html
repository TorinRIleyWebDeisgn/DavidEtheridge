Vintage C Melody Martin Stencil Saxophone

Price: $600

Condition: Good

Make / Manufacturer: Martin

Model Name / Number: C Melody

Here is a C melody saxophone built by the Martin company as a stencil for the Gretsch company. The serial number dates this to the year 1921. The engraved bell says "The Great Gretsch American". This saxophone has the soldered toneholes and opposable bell keys typical of the time period. This has recently been cleaned and re-padded - all key corks and felts are new as well. The neck is in great shape and comes with a vintage Brilhart 3-star tenor mouthpiece and ligature. Comes in a nice handmade wood case.
