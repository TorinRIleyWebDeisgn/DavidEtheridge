Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use for custom inlays?

We use high-quality materials such as mother of pearl, abalone, and exotic woods for our inlays. Each material is carefully selected to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal.

How long does a custom inlay job take?

The timeframe depends on the complexity of the design. Most inlays take 2–4 weeks to complete. We provide regular updates throughout the process to keep you informed of the progress.

Do you offer repair warranties?

Yes, we provide a 1-year warranty on all repairs and custom work. This warranty covers any defects in materials or workmanship, giving you peace of mind with your investment.
